# SkillForge

## Project Vision

SkillForge is a career guidance platform that helps students and people who want to become professionals figure out what skills they need to get their dream job.

It does not just give a plan for learning. Instead SkillForge looks at the skills the user has compares them to what the industry needs finds out what skills are missing and then creates a special plan for the user to learn with recommended resources and projects.

The main goal of the platform is to make career planning easier by giving users a plan tracking their progress and giving them practical advice that is tailored to what they want to achieve.

SkillForge will get even better in the future. It will use intelligence to look at resumes GitHub profiles, certifications and project portfolios. This will help it give users personalized advice on their careers and plans to prepare for interviews.

---

## Vision Statement

The goal of SkillForge is to become the trusted platform that helps people learn and develop their careers using artificial intelligence. We want to help every learner connect what they learn in school to what they need to know to work in their chosen field.

---

## Mission Statement

Our mission is to help students and people who teach themselves by giving them advice on their careers analyzing what skills they are missing and creating a clear plan for them to learn. We want to make it easier for people to get ready for their careers and get jobs. SkillForge is, about making career preparation simpler and helping people become more employable.
